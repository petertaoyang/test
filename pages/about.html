About

He started photography in Christmas 2011 and this passion has been the main aspect of my life since. He wanted to discover the world, so in 2013 he left all he had except his camera and planned to stay in Australia for a year, he finally ended up with and incerdible adventure: 10 months trying to hitchhike back to Europe throughout South-East Asia, alone and with barely no money! Now back in Europe, with the desire to publish and share his work through exhibitions or other projects.
